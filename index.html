FAQ
Frequently asked questions
What is GrowAlert? ▼
GrowAlert is a volunteer-run scam prevention service for the Growtopia community. We publicly list scammers and flagged traders so you can make safer deals.
How do I report a scammer? ▼
Open a report ticket in our Discord server. Bring evidence — screenshots, chat logs, and proof of payment.
What does DWC mean? ▼
DWC stands for "Deal With Caution". It indicates a user who has displayed risky or questionable trading behaviour but hasn't been confirmed as a scammer.
How do I appeal my listing? ▼
Join our Discord server and open an appeal ticket. Provide evidence supporting your case and a staff member will review it.
Can I apply for staff? ▼
Watch for announcements in our Discord server when applications are open.
